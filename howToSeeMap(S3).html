single/dual map mode

in single map mode, all kinds of marker is showed on the one map.
in dual map mode, two X-shape marker is showed on one map.
and two cross-shape marker is showed on another map.


front map/bird's-eye map

map with blue line and red line is front map.
object upper than the player is showed on upper side of map.
similarly, lower one is on lower side.
right one is on right, left one is on left.


map with yellow line and red line is bird's-eye map.
object in front of the player is showed on upper side of map.
similarly, back one is on lower side.
and right one is on right, left one is on left.
